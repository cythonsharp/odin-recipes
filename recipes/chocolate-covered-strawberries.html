Chocolate Covered Strawberries

The simplest version of chocolate-covered strawberries I know.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces milk chocolate chips
  • 2 tablespoons shortening
  • toothpicks
  • 1 pound fresh strawberries with leaves

Steps

  1. Melt chocolate and shortening in a double boiler,
    stirring occasionally until smooth.
  2. Insert toothpicks into tops (stem ends) of strawberries.
    Hold a strawberry by the toothpick and dip it
    into the chocolate mixture.
  3. Turn the strawberry upside down and insert
    the toothpick into a Styrofoam block or place
    strawberry on a sheet of waxed paper; allow
    chocolate coating to cool. Repeat with
    remaining strawberries.
